About Us

Skyview Properties Limited

Skyview Properties Limited is a trusted land development and real estate company based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We specialize in acquiring premium land parcels and developing them into quality residential flats with modern amenities. Our business model focuses on land-to-flat conversion, offering investors and residents access to affordable, well-designed apartments in prime Dhaka locations. With a deep understanding of Dhaka’s evolving real estate landscape, we focus on developing premium apartments, commercial spaces, and investment-friendly properties in prime and emerging locations. Our team of experienced engineers, architects, and project managers ensures that every development meets the highest standards of construction, safety, and sustainability. At Skyview Properties Limited, we believe in transparent operations and investor-friendly partnerships. We offer flexible investment sharing options where multiple investors can participate in land acquisition and development projects, creating accessible pathways to real estate investment and home ownership. Our vision is to become one of Bangladesh’s most trusted and recognizable real estate brands by creating landmark developments that elevate the skyline of Dhaka and enhance the lifestyle of our communities.
